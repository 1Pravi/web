About Us

KS Foundation is a registered non-government organization (Reg. No. 2/2023) committed to making a positive impact on society through various initiatives. Our mission is to support youth, foster educational development, and empower underprivileged communities. We offer scholarships and stipends to students from minority and weaker sections to help them achieve their educational goals.

Our foundation is also dedicated to social development, including:

  • Education: Providing educational support and resources.
  • Social Development: Engaging in activities that contribute to community welfare.
  • Plantation: Promoting and supporting tree planting initiatives to safeguard the environment.
  • Skill Development: Offering training and resources for skill enhancement.
  • Regular Donations: Supplying food to old age homes and supporting the homeless.
  • Animal Rescue: Assisting in the rescue and care of animals.
  • Rescue Operations: Conducting operations during floods and other emergencies.
  • Free Eye Camps: Organizing eye camps for government school students.
  • Elderly Assistance: Providing regular food donations and support for the elderly.
