Welcome to KS Foundation

We are dedicated to making a positive impact on society by supporting education, social development, and empowerment initiatives.

About Us

KS Foundation is a Non-Government organization. KS Foundation works towards youth. Our aim is to create a hunger-free society, student development, and empowerment. We grant scholarships and stipends to needy students of minority or weaker sections of society for their education. We also support and promote the growing of trees.

Our Work

  • Education
  • Social development
  • Plantation
  • Skill development
  • Regularly donating food to old age homes
  • Animal Rescue
  • Rescue operations during floods
  • Free Eye camp to Government School students
  • Rescue elder persons
  • Regular food donation for homeless people
